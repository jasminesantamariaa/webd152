Our Mission

At Pacific Trails Resort, our mission is to offer a peaceful getaway where guests can connect with nature, relax in comfort, and share unforgettable moments by the ocean. We believe that luxury and sustainability can coexist — every stay supports our ongoing effort to preserve California’s coastal trails.

Our Team

Our resort is run by a close-knit team of outdoor enthusiasts, local guides, and hospitality experts who live and breathe the Pacific lifestyle. From our friendly concierge staff to our skilled kayaking instructors, every member shares a passion for nature and genuine care for our guests.

Our Story

Pacific Trails started as a small family-owned retreat with one dream — to help people rediscover balance through the beauty of the outdoors. Decades later, our mission remains the same: to make nature accessible while maintaining the quiet charm that makes our resort feel like home.

Whether you’re hiking through the redwoods, watching the sunrise over the cliffs, or simply resting by the fire, our team is here to make your visit an unforgettable experience.
